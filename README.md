# i4-personal-risk-profile
Sovereignty Hackathon Track 2: individualized health risk profiling of Inspiration 4 crew members using multi-omics data.

This project addresses the "silent" molecular stress of spaceflight by translating the high-dimensional complexity of the SOMA/Inspiration4 atlas into actionable, individualized health intelligence. Recognizing that the current density of omics data is inaccessible for real-time mission decision-making, we developed the Personalized Health Orbit—an analytical framework that establishes unique physiological baselines for each crew member using pre-flight timepoints (L-92 to L-3). By utilizing AI to triangulate signals between OSD-575 cytokine fluctuations ("the smoke") and OSD-570 gene expression ("the fire"), we move beyond group-level averages to identify high-confidence, individualized trajectories. This approach prioritizes the magnitude of change over generalized p-values, maintaining scientific honesty regarding the n=4 sample size while providing a transparent, dashboard-style interface. The final output provides astronauts with intuitive gauges for Immune Stability, Inflammatory Load, and Recovery Velocity, ensuring that explorers maintain total sovereignty over their cellular health as they navigate the stars.
